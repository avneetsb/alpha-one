About DhanHQ

We’re trying to shape the future of trading with technology.

DhanHQ is our commitment to empowering India's trading ecosystem with advanced, low-latency infrastructure for algorithmic and automated trading. Built on the same robust technology that powers Dhan, DhanHQ offers free trading APIs, real-time market data, and a comprehensive suite of tools tailored for traders, developers, and fintech platforms. Whether you're building custom trading systems, deploying algorithmic strategies, or embedding wealth products into your product, DhanHQ provides the flexibility and speed you need. With state-of-the-art infrastructure and a developer-friendly approach, we're enabling a new era of trading innovation in India.
